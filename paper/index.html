Why Should You Recycle Paper?

  • Recycling Paper allows paper and other products to be created from the materials that were recycled.
  • Recycled paper use saves resources and reduces the paper industry's impact on the planet.
  • Because paper is made from trees, recycling helps curb deforestation and helps the planet.

Where Can You Recycle Paper?

  • Most residential recycling bins
  • Local dropoff bins
  • Note: it is important that the paper you recycle is clean and free of contaminants.
  • If recycling centers recieve contaminated materials, they will not be able to recycle them.
